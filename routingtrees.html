BGP Routing Trees

As detailed in the 2019 ACM SIGCAS COMPASS paper Borders and Gateways: Measuring and Analyzing National ASChokepoints, BGP-SAS is a suite of tools for generating Gao-Rexform model routing trees centered around particular ASes. Due to the large and growing number of ASes on the Internet this is a computationally expesnive task. Thus, we provide pre generated routing trees at the following link: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1Xarfzy7FPL-lW7Q1wD4lrjgkbdld5-Xw?usp=sharing

Additionally, the source code for BGP-SAS is available at https://github.com/KirtusLeyba/routingtree2
